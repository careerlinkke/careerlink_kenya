About CareerLink

CareerLink is a trusted job connection platform based in Nairobi, Kenya. We focus on helping job seekers find real opportunities by working closely with employers who need dedicated workers across various industries.

We simplify the job application process by offering two flexible options: direct placement or scheduled interviews. Every application is verified to ensure only genuine candidates get shortlisted.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between employers and job seekers by offering a streamlined, trustworthy, and affordable system.
